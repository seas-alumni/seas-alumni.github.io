Samanvaya
SEAS Alumni Association

VisionThe Samanvaya strives to foster a symbiotic relation between the SEAS-AU and its alumnus such that it benefits all the stakeholders.

Mission
  • To provide a platform for alumnis to connect and interact with one another.
  • To help organize workshops and guest lectures.
  • To offer projects, internships and placement opportunities.
  • To provide vital suggestions to SEAS-AU based on prevalent practices and standards in industry to better the quality of education.
